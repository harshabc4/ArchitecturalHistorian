About Me

I have a passion for architecture and how it shapes and is shaped by all the different locations in our world. My experience consists of historic preservation and cultural resource management; this includes archaelogical and historic resource surveys, conducting archival research, GIS mapping, and producing SHPO letter documentation. Additionally, I am well versed in the research, survey, and documentation of historic above ground resources with a working knowledge of federal preservation regulations, including the NRHP, Section 106, and Historic Preservation Tax Incentives.
